Business Major, Art History Minor

The business major at W&L provides a comprehensive foundation in core business principles, focusing on areas such as accounting, finance, marketing, and management. The program emphasizes critical thinking, data analysis, ethical decision-making, and effective communication, preparing students for leadership roles in a variety of industries. The art history minor at W&L offers a foundation in the study of visual culture, exploring art across diverse periods, regions, and media. The program emphasizes the connections between art, history, and society while fostering an understanding of the theoretical and ethical dimensions of art and its preservation.
